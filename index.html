About Us

WaziCare+ is a women-led, youth-powered health startup restoring dignity through maternal health care and digital healing solutions in Kenya’s underserved rural communities.

Our Mission

To bridge the health access gap for underserved women and youth in Kenya using inclusive technology and community-based support.

Our How

We bring life-saving care closer to home through mobile clinics, trauma-informed maternal support, and AI-powered tools that empower young mothers with access, knowledge, and hope.

Our Vision

A future where no woman dies giving life, and every mother, regardless of geography or income, can raise her children with strength, safety, and support.
